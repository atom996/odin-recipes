Chicken Breast

Description

Ah, chicken breast, a staple in every gym-bro's diet. Not only is it filled with protein, it is also very versatile as a meal. You can fry it, bake it, etc. This is my take on Chicken Breast:

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil (Or other fat of choice) - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Whole chicken breast
  • Black pepper - 1-2 teaspoons
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • Optional: Seasonings of choice
  • Pan (And if you have one, a lid)
  • Tongs

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, black pepper, and the other (optional) seasonings if you have them.
  2. Place pan over fire (or electric stove top) at medium heat.
  3. Wait 1-2 minutes until pan is heated.
  4. Pour fat of choice into pan.
  5. Place chicken breast into pan.
  6. Cook for 4 minutes (Keep it covered if you have a lid).
  7. Flip after 4 minutes (Cover again if you have a lid) and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  8. Plate chicken and squeeze some lemon over said chicken.
  9. Enjoy!
