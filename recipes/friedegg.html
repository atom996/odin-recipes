Fried Egg (Over Easy)

Description

Fried eggs are a good way to start your day. Eggs are not only nutritious, but they are tasty as well! My favorite way to fry eggs is over-easy. Some people prefer sunny-side up, but this is just how I prefer it. Crispy all around, runny, and delicious. Here's how to make a simple, over-easy, fried-egg:

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil (Or other fat of choice) - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Brown or White Egg (Medium/Large should do)
  • Pan
  • Spatula

Steps

  1. Place pan over fire (or electric stove top) at medium heat.
  2. Wait 1-2 minutes until pan is heated.
  3. Place fat of choice into pan and ensure that it is evenly spread over the pan.
  4. Crack the egg (or eggs if you're cooking more than one) into the pan.
  5. Sprinkle the salt over the egg(s). Note: If you do not have a way to measure 1/2 of a teaspoon, a pinch of salt should do.
  6. Fry for 30 seconds - 1 minute, or until whites are mostly cooked.
  7. Flip egg and fry for another 30 seconds - 1 minute.
  8. Optionally: Season with another pinch of salt.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
